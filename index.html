engineering

nabinchha's contributions
I like to build things that are beautiful and elegant inside out. I am very passionate about writing clean maintainable code, and using technology to better the world. I enjoy working on projects that bring value to everyday life in some way. My primary interests are in the areas of mobility, artificial intelligence, and computer vision, but I am up for solving any hard problems.

I have several years of experience contributing to existing code bases and building scalable, distributed applications taking them from application and infrastructure architecture design to development, testing, and deployment pipelines. I am very comfortable working in all layers of stack. I work at Ticketleap, where I currently focus on the engineering of Port (in beta).

I work on side projects when I have some free time. Most recently, my wife Tess and I worked on an fun game called Run the Roll and released it to the app store. It is fast-paced and challenging. It also won't cost you anything to try. Feel free to check it out!
